Events

Our Upside Event Series will shed light on various causes – one per quarter – that demand our passion. Each event will focus on a specific charity or a group that aligns with a cause we feel is crucial. By inviting our network to learn about and contribute to these causes, our hope is that we ignite the desire in our attendees to pursue the causes that resonate with them personally. The first events are predetermined, but we’ll rely on our network to share their passions with us as time goes on, and to inform the causes to illuminate for future events

Volume:One

Planned Parenthood of the Rocky Mountains

Do you support affordable access to cancer screenings? Antenatal care? Routine bloodwork? Preventative care? Surprise! You actually support the fundamental services that Planned Parenthood provides! Planned Parenthood is now the primary healthcare provider for millions of Americans, and while it’s easy for the impact of this lifeline to get lost in the rhetoric of left vs. right, now more than ever, we want to illuminate the role that Planned Parenthood serves in our communities and protect the lives that Planned Parenthood protects.

Please join us for an evening with friends, booze, bites and tunes to benefit Planned Parenthood of the Rocky Mountains. It’ll be fun, and you may just learn a thang or two while you’re at it.
