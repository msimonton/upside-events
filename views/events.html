Upside events shed light on the causes that demand our passion and attention. Each event will support a local non-profit that aligns with a cause we feel is crucial.

At Upside events, we invite the Denver community to learn about and contribute to these causes. Our hope is that attendees leave feeling educated and empowered to take action with the causes that resonate with them personally.

Annually, Upside supports Planned Parenthood of the Rocky Mountains with the Pink Party.

Why Planned Parenthood of the Rocky Mountains?

Each year, more than 100,000 women, men, and teens visit one of PPRM’s health centers. Across Colorado, Nevada, New Mexico, and Wyoming, PPRM’s life-saving services include: cancer screenings, breast exams, antenatal care, routine blood work, reproductive healthcare, and educational programming.

One third of American women are in need of publicly funded health care, and more than 60% of those women choose Planned Parenthood as their primary health care provider. For 100 years, PPRM has been an unwavering resource for the women of Colorado - including the women of Upside.

How will the Pink Party help PPRM?

PPRM relies on the philanthropic support of its communities. Last year, budget cuts forced PPRM to announce the closure of six out of its 24 health centers.

However, PPRM supporters were able to raise more than $144,000 in 2017, allowing one of the six threatened health centers to remain open! Upside’s Pink Party proudly contributed $10,000 last year, proving that together we CAN make a difference.
